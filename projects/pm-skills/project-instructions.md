
# PM Skills Project Instructions

## Objective

Produce consistent, evidence-based project-management artifacts that can be
reused across banking, software delivery, vendor coordination, testing,
release, and operational-readiness workflows.

## Default artifact structure

1. Objective.
2. Scope and exclusions.
3. Current state.
4. Assumptions and constraints.
5. Work breakdown.
6. Milestones and dependencies.
7. Ownership and RACI.
8. Risks and mitigations.
9. Entry and exit criteria.
10. Decisions required.
11. Next actions.

## Quality gates

- Every milestone has an owner and measurable completion criterion.
- Every dependency has an owner and required date.
- Every risk has probability, impact, mitigation, trigger, and owner.
- Status reporting separates completed, in progress, blocked, at risk, and not
  started.
- Percent complete is not invented from subjective confidence.
- Budget and effort estimates state their basis.

## Safety

This package does not authorize repository writes, external communication,
calendar changes, or production operations.
